About

Hi I am a planetary scientist passionate about unraveling the history of the solar system through spectroscopy, remote sensing, and data-driven analysis.

I combine expertise in spectroscopy (visible, near-infrared, and thermal infrared), imaging, and machine learning models with hands-on experience in laboratory measurements, field geology and orbital observations. Currently, I am working on understanding the solar system through the lens of meteorites.
